Occupations in IT

In 2020, the IT field is very important and technology isn’t a thing that people can live without. Therefore, there are many new job offers that have come and all of them help to create new technology. Some of these would be Software Developer or Hardware Engineer.

Software developers design a range of software solutions, from operating systems and databases to mobile applications and games. They try to understand what the software is required to do and how it will work. Developers often collaborate extensively with customers and colleagues to understand what they need You’ll need top programming skills to be a software developer - but the rewards will be worth it, because this is typically one of the best-paid graduate roles.

Hardware engineers design, build, and test computer hardware and computer systems. They also work with outlying devices such as printers, scanners, modems, and monitors, among others. Generally, hardware engineering roles become more specific as graduates move from entry-level roles into senior positions. To begin with, graduates are likely to be tasked with general duties related to the design, development, and testing of computer systems and components.
